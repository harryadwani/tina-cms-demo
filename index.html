What is a DSCR loan?
“A DSCR loan, also known as a Debt Service Coverage Ratio loan, is a specialized financing option primarily utilized by real estate investors. It measures the property’s ability to generate enough cash flow to cover its debt obligations. Unlike traditional loans focusing on personal income verification, DSCR loans evaluate the property’s income potential to determine loan eligibility. This non-QM loan allows investors to qualify for financing based on their property’s cash flow.”

How do I know if I’m eligible for a DSCR Loan?
To be eligible for a DSCR Loan, you must have a DSCR ratio of 1 or higher, i.e. your rental income should exceed your mortgage payment. Also, most lenders will require a minimum credit score of 620. Lastly, you need to pay a 20-25% down payment. You will most likely get the loan if you satisfy all these criteria.
